# Kawakib Acara

## One Platform for Your Entire Event, From First Idea to Loyal Customer

Kawakib Acara is an event management platform built for service-based businesses. If you run yoga classes, workshops, training sessions, or conferences, this is the tool that handles everything so you can focus on delivering great experiences.

Most event tools solve one piece of the puzzle. They help you sell tickets or send emails or check people in. Then you duct-tape five other tools together to cover the rest. Kawakib Acara replaces that patchwork with a single platform that covers the full event lifecycle.

---

## Who It's For

Kawakib Acara is built for organizers who run events regularly, not one-off occasions.

- **Yoga studios and fitness providers** running weekly or monthly classes
- **Workshop hosts** offering hands-on sessions with limited seats
- **Training providers** delivering multi-session programs with certificates
- **Conference organizers** managing speakers, venues, and large attendee lists
- **Community builders** running recurring meetups and events

If you create events, sell spots, show up and deliver, then follow up with your attendees afterward, this platform is for you.

---

## The Full Event Lifecycle

Kawakib Acara is structured around six stages. Each one connects to the next, so nothing falls through the cracks.

### Plan

Set up your event with everything it needs before you go public.

- Create events with multi-session catalogs and add-ons
- Set dynamic pricing: fixed rates, date-based tiers, or quantity-based tiers
- Manage venues and speakers with conflict detection
- Save event templates for recurring classes and reuse them
- Track event economics and fill rates from a single dashboard

### Market

Get the word out and fill your seats.

- Build customizable landing pages with SEO settings for each event
- Send email campaigns, reminders, and announcements
- Share to WhatsApp, X, and Facebook with one click
- Display testimonials from past attendees
- Manage waitlists for sold-out events with early bird countdown timers

### Sell

Take orders and handle payments without the spreadsheet chaos.

- Accept registrations with catalog and add-on selection
- Support manual bank transfer and QRIS payments
- Apply voucher codes and referral discounts
- Generate invoices automatically
- Track every order from pending to confirmed to completed

### Deliver

Run your event smoothly on the day.

- Check in attendees with QR codes, with undo if needed
- Track attendance per session for multi-day events
- Distribute event materials, gated behind check-in when needed
- Generate certificates automatically from your templates
- Send live announcements to checked-in attendees via email

### Follow Up

Close the loop after the event ends.

- Collect feedback and testimonials from attendees
- Run NPS surveys with flexible question types
- Send thank-you emails and certificates automatically
- Share recordings and replays with configurable availability windows
- Export financial reports to Excel

### Retain

Turn one-time attendees into regulars.

- Run a referral program where attendees earn balance for bringing friends
- Segment customers automatically by frequency, recency, and behavior
- Award loyalty credits based on attendance
- Launch re-engagement campaigns targeting specific customer segments
- Give attendees a self-service portal to manage their profile and history

## What Makes It Different

**One platform, not ten.** Most organizers juggle separate tools for landing pages, email, ticketing, check-in, and follow-up. Kawakib Acara handles all of it in one place. Your data stays connected across every stage.

**Built for repeat events.** This is not a tool for one-off concerts. It is built for businesses that run events regularly. Templates, recurring schedules, customer segmentation, and loyalty programs all reflect that.

**The lifecycle does not end at the door.** Most event tools stop at check-in. Kawakib Acara keeps going: feedback collection, surveys, certificates, referral programs, and re-engagement campaigns. The post-event experience is where retention happens.

Kawakib Acara exists because running great events should not require ten different tools and a dozen spreadsheets. Plan it, market it, sell it, deliver it, follow up, and bring people back. All in one place.
