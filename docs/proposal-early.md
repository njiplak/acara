# Kawakib Acara Early Adopter Program

## Use Kawakib Acara for Free. Help Us Build It Right.

Kawakib Acara is an event management platform built for service-based businesses: yoga studios, workshop hosts, training providers, and anyone who runs events regularly. It covers the full lifecycle from planning and marketing to selling tickets, delivering events, and retaining customers.

We are looking for a small group of early adopters to use Kawakib Acara for their real events. In return, you get full access to the platform at zero cost.

---

## What You Get

**Full platform access, no cost, no catch.**

- Create and manage events with multi-session support, dynamic pricing, and add-ons
- Build landing pages for your events with SEO settings and social sharing tools
- Sell tickets with voucher codes, referral discounts, and invoice generation
- Check in attendees with QR codes, track attendance per session
- Distribute event materials and generate certificates automatically
- Send email campaigns, reminders, and announcements to your attendees
- Collect feedback and testimonials after each event
- Run post-event surveys with NPS scoring
- Access analytics, revenue reports, and customer segmentation
- Offer membership subscriptions with monthly plans and gated resources
- Use the referral and loyalty system to keep your attendees coming back

**Priority support.** You get direct access to the founder. If something breaks or confuses you, I want to hear about it immediately.

**Shape the product.** Your feedback directly influences what we build next. Features you request get prioritized because you are the ones actually using the platform.

---

## What We Get

In exchange for free access, here is what we ask:

1. **Use it for real events.** Not a test run. Actual events with real attendees, real ticket sales, real check-ins. That is the only way we learn what works and what does not.

2. **Share honest feedback.** Tell us what is confusing, what is slow, what is missing. The more specific, the better. We will set up a simple channel (WhatsApp group or similar) for this.

3. **Report bugs when you find them.** Screenshots, screen recordings, or even a quick voice note. Anything helps.

4. **Let us observe.** Occasionally, we may ask to watch how you use the platform or ask follow-up questions about your workflow. This helps us spot friction you might not even notice.

---

## How It Works

1. **Tonight / Today:** We walk you through the platform, set up your account, and answer any questions.
2. **This week:** You start setting up your first event on Kawakib Acara.
3. **Over the next 2-3 events:** You use the platform end-to-end. We collect data, observe usage, and gather your feedback.
4. **After that:** We sit down together, review how it went, and decide on next steps.

There is no contract, no commitment beyond trying it in good faith. If it does not work for you, no hard feelings.
